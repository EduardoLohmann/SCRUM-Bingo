Scrum Bingo


This little project is all about bringing some spice into your daily meetings.
We all know that each person tends to repeat him/herself quite a lot, and that
get somewhat tiring when you have your own tasks and issues to work on.

So, since you probably can't just leave the meeting, it'd be nice to have some
way to at least make this time a little more casual. This is where the bingo comes in!

How it works

  • Insert records with people's names
  • For each person, add words they are used to use a lot when they speak
    • You can delete and modify records you alredy recorded
    • Words can reference a specific person, or everyone (use it for really generic terms like 'Meeting' or 'Code')
    • Some generic words are already built-in to ease your life, but feel free to modify however you like
  • Generate a bingo card
  • During the meeting, mark up any words said by them with the mouse
  • Thats it! See if you can score a bingo and share a screenshot with your friends!

Now, a word of advice. Use this at your own discretion. If you have trouble concentrating in meetings
or you're new at your job, maybe its not the best idea to seek distractions like this.
